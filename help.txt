1. Starting the Game:
   - When you run the game, you'll see a "Press any key to start the game" message. Simply press any key on your keyboard to begin.

2. Game Objective:
   - The objective of the game is to maneuver your car through traffic for as long as possible without crashing into other cars.

3. Controls:
   - You can control your car using the arrow keys or the "WASD" keys on your keyboard:
     - Use the left arrow key or "A" key to move left.
     - Use the right arrow key or "D" key to move right.
     - Use the up arrow key or "W" key to move up.
     - Use the down arrow key or "S" key to move down.
   - Additionally, you can use the "Z" key to activate a reverse mode and the "X" key to slow down.

4. Gameplay:
   - Once the game starts, your car will appear in the middle of the screen at the bottom.
   - Other cars (opponents) will start appearing from the top of the screen and move downwards.
   - Your goal is to dodge these cars by moving your car left, right, up, or down to avoid collisions.
   - As you progress, the speed of the cars will increase, making it more challenging to avoid collisions.

5. Scoring System:
   - You earn points as you continue driving without crashing into other cars.
   - Your current score is displayed at the top-left corner of the screen.
   - There's also a top score displayed, which represents the highest score achieved in previous games.

6. Game Over:
   - The game continues until you crash into another car.
   - When a collision occurs, the game ends, and a "Game Over" message is displayed.
   - You'll have the option to try again by pressing any key.

7. Additional Features:
   - There are some additional features in the game, such as a countdown timer at the beginning of each round and the ability to activate a reverse mode or slow down temporarily using specific keys.

8. Highest Score:
   - The game keeps track of your highest score across multiple playthroughs.
   - Your highest score is displayed alongside your current score during gameplay.

Overall, the game offers a simple yet challenging experience where players must navigate their car through traffic, avoid collisions, and aim for a high score. It's easy to pick up and play, making it suitable for players of all skill levels.
